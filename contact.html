The Girl Child Awareness Campaign welcomes individuals, schools, organizations, and communities who want to support and empower girls. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of young girls through education, awareness, and protection.


How You Can Help

You can support our movement by donating school supplies, sponsoring educational programs, volunteering at awareness events, or spreading information about the importance of protecting and educating the girl child.


Communities can also help by encouraging gender equality, supporting girls’ education, and standing against child marriage, violence, and discrimination.
